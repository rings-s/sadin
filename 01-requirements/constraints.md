# Constraints

## Introduction
This doc lists non-negotiable boundaries that shape the solution.
Keep it short and explicit to avoid hidden scope.

## Time and scope
- Releases are planned in two-week sprints.
- Critical milestones must be met for customer onboarding.

## Regulatory and compliance
- Data handling must meet privacy standards.
- Auditability is required for key actions.

## Technical constraints
- Devices may send small payloads with limited bandwidth.
- Some integrations are fixed by customer contracts.

## Budget and resources
- Cloud costs must remain within agreed limits.
- Team capacity is fixed for the quarter.

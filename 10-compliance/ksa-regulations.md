# KSA Regulations

## Introduction
This doc captures high-level compliance checkpoints for operations in KSA.
It is a working summary and must be reviewed by legal counsel.

## Compliance checkpoints
- Confirm data residency requirements for customer data.
- Maintain clear consent and notice for data collection.
- Document data access and export approvals.
- Prepare breach notification procedures.

## Evidence to keep
- Data flow diagrams and storage locations.
- Access logs and audit trails.
- Policies for retention and deletion.

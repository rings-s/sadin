# Data Privacy

## Introduction
This doc summarizes our privacy principles and operating rules.
It is a practical guide, not legal advice.

## Principles
- Collect only what we need.
- Use data for agreed purposes.
- Protect data with least-privilege access.

## Data classification
- Public: safe to share
- Internal: team-only
- Restricted: customer data and secrets

## Retention
- Telemetry retained per policy.
- Audit logs retained for compliance.

## Access rules
- Access is logged and reviewed.
- Data exports require approval.

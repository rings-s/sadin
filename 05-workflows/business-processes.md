# Business Processes

## Introduction
This doc captures non-technical workflows that affect delivery and ops.
Keep it aligned with real behavior, not just ideal process.

## Sales to onboarding
- Input: signed contract
- Output: tenant created and trained

## Support handling
- Input: customer ticket
- Output: resolution or escalation

## Change requests
- Input: requested feature or configuration
- Output: approved scope and timeline

## Billing
- Input: usage data
- Output: invoice and payment status

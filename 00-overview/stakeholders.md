# Stakeholders

## Introduction
This doc tracks the people and teams we serve, plus their needs and decision rights.
Keeping it current avoids surprises and improves alignment.

## Primary stakeholders
| Stakeholder         | Role            | Needs                 | Decision rights      | Check-in cadence |
| ------------------- | --------------- | --------------------- | -------------------- | ---------------- |
| Product             | Defines roadmap | Clear tradeoffs       | Scope and priority   | Weekly           |
| Engineering         | Builds platform | Stable requirements   | Architecture choices | Weekly           |
| Operations          | Runs platform   | Reliable alerts       | Incident response    | Weekly           |
| Security/Compliance | Manages risk    | Data handling clarity | Security controls    | Monthly          |

## Secondary stakeholders
- Sales: timelines, feature readiness
- Support: troubleshooting playbooks
- Finance: cloud cost visibility

## Communication rules
- Share risks early, not at the end.
- Decisions are documented in `08-decisions/`.
- Use a single source of truth per topic.

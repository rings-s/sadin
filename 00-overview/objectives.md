# Project Objectives

## Introduction
This doc lists the goals we are actively pursuing and how we measure them.
It helps prioritize work and tradeoffs across teams.

## Product objectives
- Make device onboarding simple and repeatable.
- Provide real-time visibility into device health.
- Deliver clear, actionable alerts.

## Business objectives
- Reduce onboarding time and support cost.
- Increase retention through reliability and trust.
- Support growth without linear ops headcount.

## Technical objectives
- Build a scalable ingestion and processing pipeline.
- Ensure data correctness and schema evolution.
- Maintain clear service boundaries and ownership.

## Operational objectives
- Meet defined availability and latency targets.
- Keep incident response consistent and fast.
- Maintain clear runbooks and dashboards.

## Review cadence
- Review objectives every sprint planning.
- Re-baseline metrics each quarter.
